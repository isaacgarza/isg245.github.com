The Life Code Inspections, Texas Government Code Section 419.908, passed by Texas Legislature in 2009, states the following:

Only an individual certified by the Texas Commission on Fire Protection as a Fire Inspector may conduct a fire safety inspection required by state or local law, rule, regulation or ordinance. In addition, it must be conducted in accordance with:

  • 1. The most recent local fire code.
  • 2. The most recent local fire code adopted by the State Fire Marshal.

    • This recent new statute went into effect on September 1, 2011 and further requires all Fire Safety Plan Reviews to be performed by a certified Fire Inspector. New policy and procedures are presently affecting many business organizations in towns and counties including nursing homes, hospitals, day care centers, schools, prisons, etc. that require a Fire Inspection and Certificates of Occupancy.
